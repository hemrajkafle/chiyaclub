Who we are

Chiya Chautari for Nepalese Canadian Youth

Chiya Club of Canada (Chiya Chautari) is a youth-driven initiative that creates safe, fun, and inspiring spaces for Nepalese Canadians to connect ‚Äì from morning fitness groups to evening chai conversations.

We believe in balancing well-being, community and career. Our activities promote physical and mental health, social connections, mentorship, and professional growth, all while keeping our Nepali roots alive.

The club is in the process of being incorporated as a non-profit organization in Canada under the legal name "Chiya Club of Canada (Chiya Chautari)".
